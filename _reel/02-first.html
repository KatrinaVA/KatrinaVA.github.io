THE VOICE YOU NEED

Katrina is an enthusiastic American voice artist who brings her fun-loving attitude and warm, girl-next-door voice to all of her clients' projects. With every project, she will find and relatably convey the emotion and passion every character or advertisement needs.

Her passion is bringing videogame and animation characters to life. Doing impressions of Disney princesses and cartoon voices for her family for years led her to her current career. Katrina always strives to achieve the best results for each project, she's never satisfied until the client is happy with her work. She has a variety of low/mid/high range voices with an array of accents that pleases all audiences. She works out of her professional home studio in the Tampa, FL area.
